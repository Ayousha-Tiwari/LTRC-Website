Foundational Strengths

Our Research Labs

Four decades of dual-competence expertise in classical linguistics and computational power. Deep understanding of Indian language structure combined with cutting-edge AI research.

NLP · Machine Translation

MT-NLP Lab

Syntax and parsing, semantics and word sense disambiguation, discourse and tree banking, machine translation, creation of linguistic resources. Computational models inspired by linguistics combined with modern machine learning.

Explore Lab →
Classical · Sanskrit

Anusaaraka Lab

Fusion of traditional Indian shastras and advanced modern technologies. Joint initiative with Chinmaya International Foundation and University of Hyderabad for Sanskrit-based machine translation and computational grammar.

Explore Lab →
Speech · Audio · Vision

Speech Processing Lab

Goal-oriented basic research in robust speech-to-text systems, natural text-to-speech systems, spoken and audio information retrieval, and biometrics using speech and video. Core speech architectures for Indic languages.

Explore Lab →
IR · IE · IA

IREL — Information Retrieval & Extraction

Fundamental research in Information Retrieval, Extraction, and Access. Search, retrieval, and extraction for complex information needs in multilingual settings. Core IR architectures and frameworks.

Explore Lab →
